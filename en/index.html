under my skin

The collection of pictures about the daily lives of those affected by chronic urticaria

In 2014, to mark the first Urticaria Day we wanted to capture moments of everyday life that can be difficult (if not impossible) for patients with chronic urticaria.

To do this, we created a collection of pictures with the goal of raise awareness and show the impact that chronic urticaria has on our quality of life.

This exhibition opened in Madrid and Barcelona on October 1, 2014 and since then has visited various cities. If you would like to have it in your city, please contact us.

Two years later, in view of the success of the pictures, we wanted to internationalize the project and open it to the rest of the world, with the intention to make ourselves known outside our borders and build relationships with other organizations. If you belong to an organization and would like to prepare an exhibition with those photographs contact us.
